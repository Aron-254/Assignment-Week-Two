Akan names are derived from Ghanaian culture.This is a culture whereby names are derived from the day of week one was born.There are specific names for male and female children.The names are listed below.

Male Names are:

Sunday: Kwasi Monday: Kwadwo Tuesday: Kwabena Wednesday: Kwaku Thursday: Yaw Friday: Kofi Saturday: Kwame

Female names are:

Sunday: Akosua Monday: Adwoa Tuesday: Abenaa Wednesday: Akua Thursday: Yaa Friday: Afua Saturday: Ama
